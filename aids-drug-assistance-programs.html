AIDS Drug Assistance Programs (ADAP)

If you are HIV+ and do not have health insurance, you may be eligible to receive your HIV/AIDS medication through your state's ADAP/Ryan White Program. ADAP is a payer of last resort, which means you must exhaust all other payment options before you can qualify. Each state has slightly different eligibility and enrollment requirements. Click on your state below for more information about your local ADAP program.
