How to use this library

The materials below mirror the Notion database and were last synced on November 13, 2025. Every record retains its original tags so you can see whether it speaks to race, gender, incarceration, cost, geography, or other intersecting determinants.

Looking for something specific? Use the keyword search to match any word in the title, subject tags, or resource type. The type filter helps you focus on policy briefs versus journal articles, podcasts, videos, or databases.
