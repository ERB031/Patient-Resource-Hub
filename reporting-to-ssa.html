Things You Must Report to SSA

If you are receiving SSI or SSDI, you must report the following within 10 days of the end of the month in which the event occurred:

  • change of address or living situation
  • changes in employment
  • changes in income or resources (or your spouse's income/resources if you are married, or your parent's income/resources if you are a minor)
  • death of anyone in your household
  • change in marital status
  • if you change your name
  • if you become a parent (including an adopted child) or if a child who was previously in your care is adopted by someone else
  • change in citizenship/immigration status or sponsorship
  • if you are receiving financial aid from friends or family
  • admission/discharge from a hospital or nursing home
  • incarceration
  • school attendance (if you are under age 22)
  • travel out of the country for over 30 days
  • if you have an outstanding warrant for your arrest, you're convicted of a crime, or you violate a condition of your parole/probation
  • improvement in your medical condition
  • if you start receiving a pension from a job for which you didn't pay Social Security taxes or if the amount of your pension changes
  • if a beneficiary dies

If you do not report one of the above changes within 10 days of the end of the month in which the event occurred, you may be:

  • underpaid or paid late
  • overpaid and required to pay the money back
  • penalties that could reduce your payments by $25 to $100
  • sanctioned for 6, 12, or 24 months

Wage Reporting

If you are earning wages, you must report them to SSI. You can use one of several wage reporting tools:

  • the SSITWR - SSI Telephone Wage Reporting system
  • the SSIMWR - SSI Mobile Wage Reporting application
  • myWageReport, the online tool within the my Social Security portal
  • sending a fax with the gross wage amount & applicable month to your local SSA office
  • mailing a letter to your local SSA office with the gross wage amount & applicable month
  • telephoning your local field office
