Awards, Grants, & Fellowships

This page mirrors our Notion doc so you can browse without leaving the site.

View original Notion doc

Awards are counted as unearned income in the month you get them. If you spend the money in the month you get it, then your SSI benefits usually are affected only in that month. Any money you keep will count as a resource for SSI purposes as long as you have it.

If you receive a grant or fellowship, money that is used for anything other than tuition, fees, and necessary educational expenses counts as unearned income in the month you received the money.
