Home & Community Based Waivers

Individuals who are elderly and/or disabled may qualify for home and community based services and/or full Medicaid coverage through one of these waiver programs:
CA Multipurpose Senior Services Program (MSSP)
Who can Apply: disabled individuals ages 65+
Financial Eligibility: must meet Medi-Cal eligibility requirements
Level of Care: must meet nursing home admission criteria.
Services:
  • Case Management
  • Personal Care Services
  • Respite Care (in-home and out-of-home)
  • Environmental Accessibility Adaptations
  • Minor Home Repair, etc.
  • Transportation
  • Chore Services
  • Personal Emergency Response System (PERS)/ Communication Device
  • Adult Day Care
  • Protective Supervision
  • Meal Services - Congregate / Home Delivered
  • Social Reassurance / Therapeutic Counseling
  • Money Management
  • Communication Services: Translation / Interpretation
Application: https://aging.ca.gov/download.ashx?lE0rcNUV0zamngby7oBeTA%3D%3D
CA Nursing Facility/Acute Hospital Waiver
Who can Apply: any age, must already be residing in a hospital or nursing facility or be at risk of institutionalization within 30 days without services from this waiver
Financial Eligibility: For individuals, the income limit is $1,481 per month and the resource limit is $2,000.
Level of Care: must need hospital or nursing home level of care
Services:
  • Case management
  • Home health aide services
  • Home modifications to enable improved access
  • Personal care
  • Medical equipment maintenance and recurring expenses
  • PERS (Personal Emergency Response service, installation and fees)
  • Respite care (both at home and in residential care on a temporary basis)
  • Skilled nursing services
  • Training for family caregivers
  • Habilitation Services
Application: https://www.dhcs.ca.gov/services/ltc/Documents/2019HCBAApp.pdf

Home & Community Based Waivers

Individuals who are elderly and/or disabled may qualify for home and community based services and/or full Medicaid coverage through one of these waiver programs:
CA Multipurpose Senior Services Program (MSSP)
Who can Apply: disabled individuals ages 65+
Financial Eligibility: must meet Medi-Cal eligibility requirements
Level of Care: must meet nursing home admission criteria.
Services:
  • Case Management
  • Personal Care Services
  • Respite Care (in-home and out-of-home)
  • Environmental Accessibility Adaptations
  • Minor Home Repair, etc.
  • Transportation
  • Chore Services
  • Personal Emergency Response System (PERS)/ Communication Device
  • Adult Day Care
  • Protective Supervision
  • Meal Services - Congregate / Home Delivered
  • Social Reassurance / Therapeutic Counseling
  • Money Management
  • Communication Services: Translation / Interpretation
Application: https://aging.ca.gov/download.ashx?lE0rcNUV0zamngby7oBeTA%3D%3D
CA Nursing Facility/Acute Hospital Waiver
Who can Apply: any age, must already be residing in a hospital or nursing facility or be at risk of institutionalization within 30 days without services from this waiver
Financial Eligibility: For individuals, the income limit is $1,481 per month and the resource limit is $2,000.
Level of Care: must need hospital or nursing home level of care
Services:
  • Case management
  • Home health aide services
  • Home modifications to enable improved access
  • Personal care
  • Medical equipment maintenance and recurring expenses
  • PERS (Personal Emergency Response service, installation and fees)
  • Respite care (both at home and in residential care on a temporary basis)
  • Skilled nursing services
  • Training for family caregivers
  • Habilitation Services
Application: https://www.dhcs.ca.gov/services/ltc/Documents/2019HCBAApp.pdf
